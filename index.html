About Me

Turning Ideas Into Visual Stories

I'm Richard Juan, a content-driven videographer based in Bandung, with a background in digital marketing and business development.

Since 2019, I've been developing my skills in photography and videography, combining creative execution with strategic thinking. I've worked on 20+ projects and managed content for multiple brands, focusing on creating video-first content that captures attention and connects with the right audience.

With experience in social media management, brand development, and content production, I don't just create visuals — I help translate ideas into content that supports brand growth. I'm passionate about building content that is not only visually strong, but also aligned with business goals and audience behavior.
